About Azaadi Studios

What is
Azaadi Studios.

Hi, I'm Sharmee and I've been building Azaadi Studios for a year now.

I aim for the studio to house a body of work in both design and process — beautiful things made beautifully. The architecture and silhouettes maintain traditional elements of universal drape-ability, flow and feel of organic materials, and the details of hand made textiles that can be shared and passed on to generations (all inherently sustainable). The design communicates my own contemporary, third-culture dance, through the textures, embroideries, craft and intimate storytelling. The process informs how craft can be created — transparent, artisan-led, locally sourced and made.

I'm learning on the way and write my progress and lessons on the blog in the journal section. My inbox is always open: hiazaadistudios@gmail.com
